The security layer for agents operating on the real web.

Guni is not a wrapper, plugin, or basic website scanner. It is the runtime policy layer between your browser agent and a hostile internet. Before an autonomous workflow clicks, submits, or follows instructions, Guni inspects the page, scores the risk, and decides whether execution is allowed, confirmed, or blocked.

What Guni becomes in a mature stack

Not a point feature. Not a checkbox. Guni becomes the control layer that product, security, and customer teams all point to when they ask a single question: why did the agent act, or why was it stopped?

InspectParse raw HTML, visible text, hidden elements, forms, scripts, buttons, and redirects before the next action fires.
ReasonBlend fast heuristics with LLM analysis so both obvious and novel attacks are caught in the same decision path.
DecideTranslate threat evidence into a policy boundary every operator can understand: allow, confirm, or block.
ProvePersist evidence, expose history, and turn blocked actions into usable audit artifacts for reviews and pilots.
